I am Valentina Guariglia, an interdisciplinary design student from Italy. I see design as a tool to create meaningful connections between people, spaces, and technology—shaping experiences that go beyond function and aesthetics.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Interior Design from Politecnico di Milano and I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Interaction Design at the University of Applied Sciences and Arts of Southern Switzerland. My practice spans across spatial, product, and interactive design, with a strong focus on crafting immersive environments and experimental installations.
