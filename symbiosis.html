Symbiosis

Interactive Installation · Art Direction · AI

Symbiosis is an interactive installation where plants take the lead in creating art and sound.
Using sensors that measure soil moisture and movement, the installation translates their natural signals into dynamic visuals and music.
First unveiled at the former ex Cementificio Saceba during an academic showcase, Symbiosis was later exhibited at Milano Design Week 2025, hosted by NAMA as part of the MiGarden collective.

Process

The development of Symbiosis involved extensive research into biomimetic design and interactive systems. The process began with studying natural growth patterns and plant responses to environmental stimuli.

Digital Plant Archive

The Symbiosis Archive is a living record of plants' changing states, where shifts in moisture or movement are transformed into visual and sound expressions. Plants become co-creators, shaping their own digital presence and turning biological rhythms into a dialogue between nature, technology, and human perception.
