The Level Group

Interior Design · Corporate · Workspace

In collaboration with LitStudio, I contributed to the renovation project of The Level Group’s offices — a key player in the global e-commerce landscape, known for crafting and managing comprehensive digital experiences for high-end luxury and design brands. I specifically designed both the custom lighting system for the staircase and hall area — transforming it into a visual focal point and guiding element within the space — and the bespoke couches structure for the break area, enriching the environment with comfort, identity, and opportunities for collaboration.
