Marine Dichotomies

Exhibition Design · Spatial Design · Curatorial

Marine Dichotomies is a temporary exhibition conceived and designed, both at a spatial and curatorial level, whose theme is the duality of the situation of current marine environments: the beauty of marine flora and fauna that are in danger due to climate change and pollution of the seas.
The constant, as the title suggests, is the design choice to develop the exhibition through contrasts: the analysis of the beauties and the denunciation of the present threats.

Exhibition Pavilion

The entire pavilion takes the form of a giant sculptural wave, a fluid and enveloping architecture that immediately recalls the strength and fragility of the sea. Its undulating shape guides visitors in a continuous flow, as if being drawn inside the ocean itself.
