Bliss

Product Design · UX/UI · Wellness

Bliss is a wearable personal assistant designed to support people dealing with recurrent cystitis, a condition that is often invisible but can deeply affect quality of life, especially for active young women. Bliss responds to this gap by acting as a personal health companion, focusing on prevention, empowerment, and lifestyle integration. Its wearable form makes it always accessible, while its multimodal interaction—voice, light, sound, and app—ensures flexibility and inclusivity.
