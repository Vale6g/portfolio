Words & Works

Exhibition Design · Installation · Curatorial

Words & Works is an exhibition I worked on during my internship at Design Differente. Held at the Fab Lab during Design Week 2023, it featured paper sculptures exploring renewable energy and environmental impact.
The event was promoted by ENEA, the National Agency for New Technologies, Energy, and Sustainable Economic Development.

Soft Sculptures

Venus de Milo

I reinterpreted Salvador Dalí's iconic Venus de Milo with drawers through a 3D model translated into more than 800 layers of cardboard, laser-cut and hand-assembled. The drawers were 3D printed and integrated into the structure, creating a piece that merges craftsmanship, digital experimentation, and art historical reference.
