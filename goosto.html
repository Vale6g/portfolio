Goosto

Digital Experience · UX/UI · Branding

Goosto is an ethnic restaurant discovery app that connects food lovers with authentic culinary experiences around them and beyond. More than just a dining guide, it empowers restaurant owners to share the heart and soul of their cuisine—the stories, traditions, and cultural heritage behind every dish—while offering users AI-driven recommendations to explore new flavors and communities. Through storytelling and community interaction, Goosto transforms dining into a journey of cultural discovery.

Primary Research

Through our interviews and survey analysis, we discovered that users are increasingly interested in authentic cultural experiences when dining out.
Many participants expressed frustration with the lack of cultural context and storytelling in their dining experiences, highlighting an opportunity for our platform to bridge this gap.

User Target

AI Integration

Goosto uses a team of intelligent agents—each with its own role. They collaborate in real-time to guide choices, adapt to user needs, and grow with every interaction.

Through an AI-guided storybuilding process, restaurant owners are supported in crafting their cultural narrative using a conversational interface. On the other side, AI-powered storytelling helps users discover the stories, traditions, and people behind each restaurant, turning every meal into a cultural experience.
